Research


Currently I am working on the generalization ability of deep neural networks, trying to answer why deep learning works so well in real world problems. In particular, I study the generalization of convolutional neural networks (CNN) on some high dimensional generative models, such as generative adversarial nets (GAN). In addition to machine learning, I am also interested in some emerging issues of data science, including data anonymity or privacy . For instance, I have studied the fundamental limits of anonymous detection or inference, with application in crowdsourced machine learning or sensor networks.
